Sour Coleslaw

Descripton

A tangy sour coleslaw recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1/4 Cup White Wine Vinegar
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 8 Cups Cabbage
  • 1 Teaspoon Dill Weed
  • 1 Teaspoon Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl
  2. Let sit for at least 3 hours in the refridgerator
