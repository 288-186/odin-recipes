Potato Salad

Descripton

This recipe is right out of my Grandmothers cookbook. I think is the best there is. It's a plain and simple potato salad, but it's delicious!

Ingredients

  • 6 Red Potatoes (boiled)
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 3 Large Celery Stalks
  • 5 Hard Boiled Eggs
  • 1 Teaspoon Celery Seed
  • 1/4 Cup Sour Pickle Relish
  • 1 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Pepper
  • 1/2 Cup Mayo
  • 1/2 Cup Spicy Mustard
  • 1 Tablespoon White Wine Vinegar
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Parsley
    Mix all ingredients together and refridgerate for 4 hours.
